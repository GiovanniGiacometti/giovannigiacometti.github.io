About

I currently work as a Machine Learning Engineer at ML cube, a Politecnico di Milano spin-off, where I’m involved in the development of the ML cube Platform, an MLOps platform that offers data and model monitoring, drift explainability and performance-safe retraining.

I hold a Master’s degree in Artificial Intelligence from Politecnico di Milano and a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from Università Politecnica delle Marche. During the second year of the Master, I had the opportunity to spend a semester at the IT University of Copenhagen as an Erasmus student, which was an incredibly enriching experience.

I enjoy building things, both related to AI and not. I like sharing things I know and learning from others. I love the feeling of being able to transform ideas into reality. I also believe that as developers we have to do our job responsibly.

  • In November 2024, I obtained the Google Cloud Professional Machine Learning Engineer certification.
